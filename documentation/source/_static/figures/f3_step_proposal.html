fanroots.step_proposal — Methods
Inputs
Current state (h, F, J)
$F(h)$ residual  ·  $J(h)$ Jacobian (shape $m \times n$)  ·  momentum, lr
Complex F, J: stack [Re; Im] rows before solving
Step direction
Gauss-Newton / Newton
$\min_p \|Jp + F\|_2$
QR-pivoted lstsq (scipy gelsy)
Levenberg-Marquardt
$[J;\,\sqrt{\lambda}D]\,p = [-F;\,0]$
dynamic $\lambda$: ÷ν on success, ×ν on failure
Gradient descent
$p = -J^T F$
steepest descent of $\tfrac{1}{2}\|F\|^2$
Output
Step direction p
shape (n,) — passed to step_size optimiser for scaling by α·m·η
